About Me


I am a Senior Software Engineer at HSBC, where I work on Machine Learning and Distributed Computing. Being absolutely passionate about Backend Engineering, Distributed Systems and Machine Learning, I thoroughly enjoy building highly scalable systems and leveraging machine learning for process optimization.

At HSBC, my team and I are developing an internal data science platform to provide Machine Learning as a Service (MLaaS) for seamless training and deployment of models. Prior to this, I was working with the Microservices and API team. I have also had the fortunate opportunity to work as a Research Intern at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur. In 2019, I graduated with a bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from the National Institute of Technology, Surat.

Being a huge fan of motorsport, I follow Formula One (Go AMG!) - which means I would probably be unreachable on a Grand Prix weekend. When I'm not working, I'm either travelling or eating burgers and rewatching 'The Office'.
